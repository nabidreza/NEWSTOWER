About NEWSTOWER

This article is about a daily newspaper in Bangladesh & World wide. For other uses, see NewsTower.

The NewsTower is the largest circulating daily English-language newspaper in Bangladesh. Founded by Nabid Reza on 13 January 2022, as Bangladesh transitioned and restored parliamentary democracy, The NewsTower is considered a newspaper of record for Bangladesh.

Its motto, "Committed to People's Right to Know", appears on the above the fold middle section of the front page. The newspaper is led by Nabid Reza. Its principal bureaus are located in Gazipur. It is organized into sections: News, Opinion and Editorial, Arts and Entertainment, National and Metropolitan. Its major supplements include The Star, Forum, Lifestyle, Star Literature, Law & Our Rights, Shout, Star Insight and Science and Life.
